# Texas Trucking Market – Interactive Marketing Dashboard Report

## Objective
This report documents the purpose, structure, and business value of the Texas Trucking Companies Interactive Dashboard built using FMCSA (Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration) Company Census data. The primary objective of this dashboard is to enable the marketing and growth teams to:

- Understand the size and structure of the Texas trucking market
- Identify high-opportunity segments for outreach and paid marketing
- Support dataSA-driven decisions for campaign targeting, budgeting, and channel strategy
- Provide a reusable, auditable analytical asset suitable for internal stakeholders and public portfolio use (GitHub)

## Data Source
Dataset: FMCSA MCMIS Company Census
Scope Applied:
- State filter: Texas (TX)
- Unique identifier: DOT_NUMBER
The FMCSA Company Census contains registration, operational, and safety-related attributes for all federally registered motor carriers in the United States.

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is designed as an interactive, self-service analytical tool with slicers, tables, KPIs, and trend visuals. Users can dynamically filter by city and explore market composition across geography, company characteristics, and time.

## Key Questions the Dashboard Answers
How many trucking companies operate in Texas?
Where are trucking companies geographically concentrated?
What types of carrier operations dominate the market?
What does the fleet-size distribution look like?
How many companies handle hazardous materials?
How has new company formation changed over time?
Which segments represent the best marketing opportunities?

## How Marketing Teams Can Use This Dashboard
### Lead Targeting
Filter by new authorities + small fleets + active status
Export ranked lists for sales and outreach

### Campaign Planning
Allocate Google Ads budget to high-density & high-growth cities
Adjust messaging based on carrier operation type

### Channel Strategy
Owner-operators → Paid search & outbound
Mid-size fleets → Email + retargeting
Large fleets → Enterprise sales & partnerships

## Conclusion
This dashboard transforms raw FMCSA registration data into a decision-support system for marketing strategy, lead generation, and market analysis. It enables stakeholders to move from descriptive reporting to actionable targeting, ensuring marketing resources are deployed where they generate the highest return.
